Rajagiri Hospital, established in 2014 was a thoughtful initiative from the renowned Rajagiri (CMI) group of institutions to offer quality and affordable medical care to the common man. The legacy of Rajagiri in pioneering a distinctive culture in the educational sector has helped them carve an exemplary medical institution in Kerala. The 500 bedded hospital is well laid out in a 40-acre green landscape in the serene setting of Chunangamvely, Aluva, and is the closest facility to the Cochin International Airport.Â
